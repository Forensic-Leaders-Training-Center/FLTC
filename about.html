About FLTC
Our Strengths

Affordability - We're on a mission to democratize leadership development with affordable programs, courses, and coaching, all without sacrificing quality. You shouldn't have to pay for executive-level training that doesn't fit your position. We want to provide quality service that meets you where you are.

Flexibility - We know crime scenes don't occur on a regular schedule, and rush cases can come into the lab at any time. That's why we're offering a flexible and comprehensive leadership development solution that's easily accessible at any time and from anywhere in the world.

Community - You are part of a unique professional community that's doing challenging work. We want to bring you and like-minded peers together with a network that is welcoming and supportive.

Personalization - You are a unique person, with individual needs, goals, and challenges at work. That's why we're offering you the chance to work with a coach who can guide you through those tough times and situations that are uniquely yours.

Meet the Team


Our Mission

We believe that leadership is a continuous journey rather than a single destination. Our mission is to empower forensic professionals with lifelong leadership skills, whether at the bench or in the lab director's chair. By improving the leadership abilities of professionals who influence the criminal justice system, we commit to enhancing and supporting the public good.

Meet Our Mission Partners


Our Vision

Our vision is a system of collaborative forensic laboratories that promotes the open exchange of scientific information and debate. This collaboration will eventually provide the public with valid, reliable, accurate, and well-communicated forensic services to the criminal justice community.

We aim to continuously improve leadership in the forensics industry by equipping aspiring leaders with the confidence and skills to advance in their careers and excel in their roles with more accessible leadership development solutions.


Our Values
Flexibility in Leadership. Trust through Consistency.

  • Flexibility
    We cultivate a culture of adaptability and flexibility in our course offerings and services, recognizing that individual needs may vary. The ability to adjust quickly is an essential leadership skill.
  • Leadership
    Effective leadership encompasses more than superior people management or having all the answers. We aim to help our members develop self-awareness and a comprehensive skillset to become well-rounded leaders.
  • Trust
    The relationships between forensic practitioners, the criminal justice community, and law enforcement require robust public trust. Effective leadership is crucial to a forensic laboratory's success, as trust is integral to the efficacy of the criminal justice process, impacting citizens' lives and well-being.
  • Consistency
    Consistency between words and actions is a hallmark of authentic and integral leadership, demonstrating credibility and reliability to others.

Our Community

The forensic science community is a unique network of professionals who work together to solve complex problems related to crime and justice. To create a thriving community that supports the needs of forensic scientists, we bring these professionals together with membership at FLTC.

Membership benefits include:

  • A personal profile and directory listing to allow members to contact and network with one another
  • Access to a private forum where members can discuss issues related to forensic science and leadership, share resources, and exchange ideas
  • Discounts on periodical Leadership Skills Guides that provide foundational learning in a particular leadership skill
  • Free coaching assessment calls and discounts on their regular coaching sessions
  • Access to our comprehensive leadership development program
  • Access to exclusive events such as webinars, workshops, and leadership academies hosted by forensic and leadership experts

Our Coaching

Our coaching program is designed to provide credentialed and experienced leadership coaches who can offer guidance, support, and advice to professionals in the forensics industry. Our coaches are focused on helping forensic scientists develop the leadership skills necessary to navigate the complexities of their industry, advance their careers, and make a positive impact in their organizations and communities.

Coaching services are provided on a one-on-one basis and cater to the individual needs and preferences of our clients.

Coaching sessions are conducted via video conferencing, allowing our clients to participate from anywhere in the world. Each coaching session is focused on specific goals and outcomes, with a clear action plan established at the end of each session to help clients stay on track and achieve their objectives.

Our Academies

Our planned FLTC Leadership Academies are intensive, week-long programs that instruct participants on the skills essential to forensic leadership. These academies will primarily take place in-person; remote options will be available for those who cannot travel to attend in-person.

Attending these academies will provide our participants with the recognition of being a forensic leader. Furthermore, participants may elect to take a certification exam at the end of the week for the latter two academies:

  • Basic Leadership Academy (Planned Launch: Q4 2024)
  • FLTC-Certified Forensic Leader (FLTC-CFL) Academy (Planned Launch: Q4 2025)
  • FLTC-Certified Forensic Executive (FLTC-CFE) Academy (Planned Launch: Q4 2026)

The Basic Leadership Academy is for all personnel - students, bench-level criminalists, and others - who want to get started on becoming better leaders.

The FLTC-CFL certification is primarily for technical leads, supervisors, and other mid-level management personnel.

The FLTC-CFE certification is primarily for lab directors, assistant lab directors, division chiefs, and other high-level management personnel.

The FLTC-CFL certification is a prerequisite to testing for the FLTC-CFE certification.
